CARLON Equine Visite

For the vet and the clinic. Your patients' recordings, made readable.
The owner sends the file, you open it in the browser — no upload, no login. Course over weeks against the horse's own baseline, a triage board that puts the conspicuous horse first, route, logbook and findings PDF. The data stays in your folder; the browser only makes it legible.

CARLON Equine und CARLON Equine Visite sind ein Paar. Halter misst → Datei → Tierärztin liest.
